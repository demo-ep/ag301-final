Fire Information for Resource Management System

FIRMS is a service that provides satellite imagery to support strategic fire management.

It is a joint project between NASA and the USDA Forest Service.

A Fire Data API is available to analyze fire regions.

Facilitating Rapid Decision-Making

Research used 947 fires:

  • classified as uncontrolled, forest, natural, and peat fires
  • lasting between 1 and 3 months
  • Covering an area between 0.5 - 150 square kilometers

By learning how a fire spreads under known conditions, prescribed burning plans can be created with greater confidence.

Setting residents at ease with proper planning will encourage future burns.

Models can be used to demostrate how local burning could improve the landscape.
