Fire Information for Resource Management System

FIRMS is a service that provides satellite imagery to support strategic fire management.

It is a joint project between NASA and the US Forest Service.

A Fire Data API is available to analyze fire regions.

Facilitating Rapid Decision-Making

Create new models to predict fire behavior based on weather and fuel conditions.

Further research is needed to better predict fire behavior using existing models.

Use Wildfire Models

Models can be used to demostrate how local burning could improve the landscape.

Setting residents at ease with proper planning will enable future burns.
